🌟 About Smart Study Hub

Why we created this platform and who it’s for

🎯 Our Mission

Smart Study Hub was built for students who want academic support but can’t always access on-campus academic centers. Whether you're commuting, balancing work, or just need help on your own time, this platform is here for you.

💡 What We Offer

  • Interactive AI to answer basic academic questions
  • Study tools and resources available 24/7
  • A future hub for productivity, organization, and success

🙌 Built by Students, for Students

This site was created as part of a college project with one goal: to make studying more accessible, especially for those who don’t always have access to in-person support.
